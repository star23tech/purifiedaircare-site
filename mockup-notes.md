## Brand / Color Scheme

- Palette matched to the company's Google Business Profile logo: deep navy background, bright EKG green (`#34d859`), cyan accent (`#22c7e6`), and a blue heart (`#3f74ff`).
- The hero "logo card" and header mark recreate the logo's heartbeat wordmark and the "Medical-Grade Duct Cleaning" tagline in SVG/text.
- The actual mascot artwork (duck) is NOT embedded — the pasted image was not available as a file. Drop the real logo in at `assets/logo.png` (or re-send it as a file) to place it on the site.

## Assumptions / Placeholders

- The mascot logo art is not embedded yet; the hero card is a wordmark recreation standing in for it.
- No AI-generated images were used.
- Gallery boxes are clean placeholders for future approved real photos, before/after images, equipment photos, or social proof.
- The contact form is visual only and not connected.
- Dryer vent safety language is intentionally cautious and avoids unsupported claims.
- No pricing is included.
- Owner name and personal rapport details are intentionally omitted from visible site copy.

## Design Choices

- Local, practical, clean, and not overly corporate.
- Phone-first calls to action for a service business that likely receives direct calls and referrals.
- Blue/teal clean-air palette with a warm accent feel suitable for duct cleaning, dryer vent cleaning, and indoor airflow.
- Design-forward hero section emphasizes the requested collateral angle: logo-refresh direction, door hangers, social graphics, and future website consistency.
- Sections included: services, local trust/social proof, simple 3-step process, gallery placeholders, and contact/estimate path.

## What This Mockup Improves

- Gives Purified Air Care an owned-web presence concept beyond Facebook.
- Makes the phone number and estimate path obvious on mobile.
- Organizes duct cleaning and dryer vent cleaning into simple customer-facing service blocks.
- Creates a place for real photos, reviews, and Facebook proof.
- Establishes a consistent brand direction that could extend to print and social graphics.
